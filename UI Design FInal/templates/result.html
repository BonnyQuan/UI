Quiz Result

Question 1: What is the primary purpose of a cash flow statement?

Your answer: C. To detail the company's sales figures for the fiscal year.

Correct answer: B. To provide information about the company's cash receipts and cash payments during a period.

Question 2: Which section of the cash flow statement reports cash flows from borrowing and repaying loans?

Your answer: A. Cash flows from operating activities.

Correct answer: C. Cash flows from financing activities.

Question 3: What does a positive cash flow from operating activities indicate?

Your answer: B. The company is experiencing a cash deficit.

Correct answer: A. The company generates more cash from its core business operations than it spends.

Question 4: How are cash flows from investing activities typically categorized?

Your answer: D. Cash inflows only.

Correct answer: B. Cash inflows and outflows related to buying and selling investments or assets.

Question 5: What is free cash flow?

Your answer: B. The total amount of cash and cash equivalents.

Correct answer: D. The cash a company generates after accounting for capital expenditures.

Question 6: What is the operating cash flow ratio?

Your answer: C. The ratio of cash flows from financing activities to cash flows from operating activities.

Correct answer: A. The ratio of operating cash flow to current liabilities.

Question 7: How do you calculate the cash flow to debt ratio?

Your answer: A. Divide operating cash flow by total liabilities.

Correct answer: D. Divide operating cash flow by total debt.

Question 8: Which of the following is a key metric derived from cash flow statements?

Your answer: A. Earnings per share.

Correct answer: C. Cash flow from operations.

Question 9: Why is it important to analyze cash flow data?

Your answer: D. To determine the company's market share.

Correct answer: B. To evaluate the company's liquidity and solvency.

Question 10: What should you look for when analyzing cash flow trends?

Your answer: C. Consistency in dividend payments.

Correct answer: A. Patterns over multiple periods.
